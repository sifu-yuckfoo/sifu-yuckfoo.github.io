About AIG

Operational Philosophy & Leadership

Akamai Intelligence Group (AIG) is a specialized R&D firm focused on the intersection of distributed systems architecture and advanced autonomous governance. We build frameworks that allow multi-agent systems to operate securely in contested, degraded, and communication-denied environments.

Christopher Ramos

Principal Investigator / Lead Systems Architect

Christopher Ramos brings a highly unconventional and empirically grounded methodology to distributed systems engineering: Culinary Operations Analysis.

Drawing on deep professional expertise as an Executive Chef and restaurant operations system designer, Ramos adapts the high-pressure, decentralized organizational paradigm of the classic Kitchen Brigade into formal software coordination architectures. In high-volume culinary operations, complex tasks are executed asynchronously by specialized nodes (stations) without constant top-down micromanagement. The environment itself—through tickets, physical staging, and standardized operational procedures (SOPs)—acts as a stigmergic state bus.

This biological and operational reality forms the foundation of AIG's approach to distributed synthetic cognition. By mapping the efficiency, fault-tolerance, and modular redundancy of brigade-based execution onto multi-agent AI, AIG develops systems that organically resist structural collapse and adversarial contamination.
